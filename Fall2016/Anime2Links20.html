Node area is proportional to Pop. Closer Nodes have more respondents watching both.

Nodes only included if they have more than a 2% Pop.

Links have a 20% threshold. This is a relative average.

Ex. 16.1% of 3-gatsu no Lion watchers watched Ajin 2, while 49.4% of Ajin 2 watchers watch 3-gatsu no Lion. The average of that is over the threshold.
